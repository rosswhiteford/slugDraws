Slug Draws

Artist

Jamie Kerr (Slug Draws) is an artist based in Edinburgh, Scotland, working in mixed-media. Pen & pencil sketches, photography, and digital image manipulation are brought together to create images of everyday life that are both familiar yet alien to our eyes.
