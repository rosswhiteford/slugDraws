About the artist

Jamie Kerr is a mixed-media artist living in Edinburgh, Scotland. His work has appeared online and in local galleries, and has been featured as cover art for published novels and promotional material for touring musicians. Jamie draws his inspiration from science fiction, local architecture, and the natural world; melding together hand-drawn sketches with photographs and digital imagery.

The artist's work is available for sale in a variety of mediums. Please contact Jamie directly for commission requests or exhibition inquiries.
